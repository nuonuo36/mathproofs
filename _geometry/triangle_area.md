## Equilateral Triangle
Area of equilateral triangle = $$\frac{\sqrt{3}}{4}s^2$$
### Proof 1 
Use **Pythagorean Theorem** and **30-60-90** special triangle

Let the side-length of an equilateral triangle be $$s$$, so
altitude = a = $$\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}s$$
base = b = $$s$$
area = A

\begin{align*}
A &= $$\frac{a\cdotb}{2}$$
&=$$\frac{\sqrt{3}}{4}s^2$$
\end{align*}
