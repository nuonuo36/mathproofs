## Sum

The sum of an arithmetic squence is always: **number of terms $$\cdot$$ average of terms**

* If $$d=1$$:

number of terms = $$a_n - a_1 +1$$ 

average of terms = $$\frac{a_n+a_1}{2}$$

Sum = $$(a_n - a_1 +1)\cdot(\frac{a_n+a_1}{2})$$
